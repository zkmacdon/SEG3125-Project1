Our Services:

Medical Dermatology

We offer the full range of medical services, including the diagnosis and treatment of acne, rosacea, as well as the diagnosis and removal of both benign and malignant skin lesions. Our physicians are experts in the diagnosis and minimally-invasive removal of skin lesions presenting in darker skin tones.

Cosmetic Dermatology

We offer services in hyperpigmentation treatment, scarring, and more with the use of microneedling, laser therapy, chemical peels, and topical medication. Our dermatologists have fellowship training in cosmetic dermatology, and have extensively researched the best treatments for scarring and pigmentation in darker skin tones. We also offer a wide variety of injectibles for scar treatment, anti-aging, and more.

Because our medical dermatology services are covered by OHIP, there is no fee associated with any of our medical dermatology services -- just make sure to bring your health card with you!
